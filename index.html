Overview

Toothless, the Scout Bot V1.0, is more than just a robot; it is a testament to resilience, innovation, and collaboration. Named after a personal experience, Toothless is designed to navigate and explore challenging terrains, making it a valuable tool for disaster response and infrastructure assessment in Bangladesh.

Project Objectives

Toothless was created with several key objectives in mind:

  • Scout and Navigate: Designed to explore and traverse challenging terrains where human access is difficult or impossible.
  • Data Collection for Disaster Response: Equipped to collect crucial data on environmental hazards such as temperature, water levels, and harmful gases.
  • Infrastructure Assessment: Post-disaster, Toothless can assess structural damage, aiding in efficient rebuilding efforts.
Hardware Overview

Chassis: A sturdy wooden chassis designed to balance weight, durability, and flexibility.

Arduino Uno #1: Controls the Bluetooth module and motor control for navigation.

Arduino Uno #2: Manages sensors such as ultrasonic, liquid level, gas, and flame sensors.

Rocker Bogie Mechanism: Provides enhanced mobility on uneven terrain.

Power System: Powered by 11.1v LiPo batteries and a power bank for portable and compact energy.

Design Considerations

The design of Toothless was carefully planned using TinkerCAD, ensuring a balance between functionality and cost-effectiveness. The wooden chassis and rocker bogie suspension system are crafted to withstand rough terrains, making Toothless both durable and agile.

The integration of QR codes and a unique audio introduction enhances user interaction, allowing easy access to the circuit design and operational instructions.

Future Integrations

Looking ahead, several upgrades are planned to enhance Toothless's capabilities:

  • ESP32 for Autonomy: Integrating ESP32 to provide autonomy and enhance the robot's vision capabilities.
  • GPS Tracker: Adding a GPS tracker to improve navigation accuracy and reliability.
  • Flight Capabilities: Aspiring to give Toothless morphing capabilities to enable flight, making it a versatile tool for various scenarios.
Impact on Bangladesh

Toothless directly addresses the unique challenges faced by Bangladesh, particularly in disaster-prone areas. By providing a sophisticated means to navigate difficult terrains and gather critical data, Toothless significantly advances disaster preparedness and recovery efforts, setting a new standard for innovative disaster response strategies.
